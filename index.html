conclusion of 2023

Last year, I made a lot of mistakes, sins, failures, I know I'm aware. but from all those mistakes, it makes me believe that wherever and whenever I am, whether it's in this year or the next, or even in this time, I can become someone better. 2023 is the year I learn more about myself, the lazy, the procrastinator, the slow, the greedy, that's me but on the other hand I'm the dreamer, the logician, the imaginative, the empath, and that's also me. I'm not a neat, genius and organized person even so I'm sure that I, the trickster, can surpass myself in the past and get through the obstacles that exist.
Happy new year and welcome to 2024
